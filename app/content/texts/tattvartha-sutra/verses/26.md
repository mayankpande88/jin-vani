transliteration: |

simple_hi: 'अवधि और मनःपर्यय के बीच विशुद्धि, क्षेत्र, स्वामी, और विषय — इन चार के द्वारा भेद है।'

simple_en: 'The difference between avadhi and manaḥparyaya is in four respects: *viśuddhi* (purity), *kṣetra* (region/range), *svāmi* (possessor), and *viṣaya* (object).'

translation_hi: 'अवधि और मनःपर्यय में विशुद्धि, क्षेत्र, स्वामी और विषय — इन चारों दृष्टि से भेद है।'

bhavarth_hi: 'अवधि-ज्ञान (दिव्य-दर्शन) और मनःपर्यय-ज्ञान (मन-पर्याय का ज्ञान) — इन दोनों में चार अन्तर:\n\n1. **विशुद्धि** — मनःपर्यय अधिक शुद्ध है।\n2. **क्षेत्र** — अवधि का क्षेत्र विस्तृत (समस्त लोक तक); मनःपर्यय का क्षेत्र मनुष्य-लोक तक सीमित।\n3. **स्वामी** — अवधि देव/नारकी/मनुष्य/तिर्यञ्च सभी को सम्भव; मनःपर्यय केवल उन्नत मुनियों को।\n4. **विषय** — अवधि सब रूपी (मूर्त) पदार्थों को जानता है; मनःपर्यय केवल मन के पर्यायों (मानसिक स्थितियों) को।'

translation_en: 'The difference between *avadhi* and *manaḥparyaya* is in four respects: *viśuddhi* (purity), *kṣetra* (region of operation), *svāmi* (possessor — who can have it), and *viṣaya* (object — what it knows).'

meaning_en: |
  Distinguishes the third and fourth jnānas across four axes:

  - **Viśuddhi**: manaḥparyaya is purer / clearer than avadhi.
  - **Kṣetra**: avadhi&apos;s range can extend across the entire universe; manaḥparyaya&apos;s range is limited to the *manuṣya-loka* (the human realm).
  - **Svāmi**: avadhi is available to celestial beings, infernal beings, humans, and animals; manaḥparyaya only to highly advanced human ascetics.
  - **Viṣaya**: avadhi knows all *rūpī* (material) objects; manaḥparyaya knows only the mental modes of other beings.

  Note: although avadhi has wider reach, manaḥparyaya is held to be qualitatively superior because of greater purity. Range and depth are different things.
