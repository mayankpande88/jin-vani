  tattvārtha-śraddhānaṁ samyag-darśanam

simple_hi: 'सात तत्त्वों के सच्चे स्वरूप पर श्रद्धा रखना — यही सम्यग्दर्शन है।'

simple_en: 'Faith in the true nature of the seven categories (*tattvas*) — this is right faith.'

translation_hi: 'तत्त्वों के अर्थ में श्रद्धा सम्यग्दर्शन है।'

bhavarth_hi: 'सम्यग्दर्शन की परिभाषा। श्रद्धा अर्थात् "सच यही है" का दृढ़ निश्चय। और किस पर श्रद्धा? तत्त्वार्थ — सात तत्त्वों के यथार्थ स्वरूप पर। (अगले सूत्र 1.4 में सात तत्त्व बताए जाते हैं।) यह श्रद्धा अंधी नहीं — समझ-आधारित है।'

translation_en: 'Faith / conviction in the meaning of the *tattvas* (categories of reality) is right faith.'

meaning_en: |
  This sutra defines the first component of the path — *samyag-darśana*. It is *śraddhāna* — faith — in *tattvārtha* — the meaning of the categories.

  Two things to note. First: the faith is in *categories*, not in a person or a story. The Tattvartha tradition will list the seven tattvas in 1.4 — soul, non-soul, influx, bondage, stoppage, shedding, liberation. Faith means accepting the reality of these as the structure of existence. Second: this is not blind faith. *Tattvārtha-śraddhānam* literally means faith based on the meaning of the categories — i.e., faith that has *understood* what it has faith in. Sarvarthasiddhi distinguishes *samyag-darśana* (which is grounded) from *mithyā-darśana* (which is uncritical or wrong belief).
