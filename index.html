About Jin-vani

A Digambara Jain reading library — built by a community member for the community. Texts are presented in their Digambara recensions with translations and meanings in Hindi and English.

MVP build · Content draft pending Digambara cultural reviewer

Project status

Draft.All content is awaiting review by the Digambara community. Until that's complete, treat translations and interpretive framings as provisional.

Built by a single Digambara practitioner. Not a wiki. Not authoritative. Use, but verify against your trusted sources.
