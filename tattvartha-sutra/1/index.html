Verse १
सम्यग्दर्शन-ज्ञान-चारित्राणि मोक्ष-मार्गः॥१॥

samyag-darśana-jnāna-cāritrāṇi mokṣa-mārgaḥ

Simple Meaning · सरल अर्थ

सम्यक्-दर्शन (सही श्रद्धा), सम्यक्-ज्ञान (सही जानकारी), और सम्यक्-चारित्र (सही आचरण) — ये तीनों मिलकर मोक्ष का मार्ग हैं।

Right faith, right knowledge, and right conduct — these three together are the path to liberation.

Sources

  • 1. — Acharya Pujyapada — extensive commentary establishing why all three components must coexist for the path to function. The order (faith → knowledge → conduct) is also explained.
  • 2. — Manatunga: tvam-eva samyag-upalabhya jayanti mṛtyuṁ — only those who truly comprehend (samyak) overcome death. The same word.
