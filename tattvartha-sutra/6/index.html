Verse ६
प्रमाण-नयैरधिगमः॥६॥

pramāṇa-nayair-adhigamaḥ

Simple Meaning · सरल अर्थ

तत्त्वों का यथार्थ ज्ञान दो साधनों से होता है — प्रमाण (पूर्ण ज्ञान-साधन) और नय (एक-एक दृष्टि से देखना)।

The (true) knowledge of the categories is obtained through *pramāṇa* (means of valid knowledge in their fullness) and *naya* (partial standpoints, viewpoints).

Sources

  • 1. — Pujyapada — definition of pramāṇa and naya; classification of nayas. The seven-naya system that developed in later Jain philosophy is foundational here.
  • 2. — Kundakunda elaborates the perspectival method extensively, distinguishing absolute (niścaya-naya) and conventional (vyavahāra-naya) viewpoints.
