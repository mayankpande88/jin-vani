Taranga

तारंगा

State: GujaratDistrict: MehsanaNearest town: KhedbrahmaCoordinates: 24.1300°N, 72.7800°E

Hill with temples of both Shvetambara and Digambara traditions. The Shvetambara Ajitanatha temple (12th c.) is notable for its scale.
